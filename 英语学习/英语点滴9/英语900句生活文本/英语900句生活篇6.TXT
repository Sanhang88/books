5.Passage 短文
(1)Language
  Miss Alice Green is an international student in our class.She comes from Australia.Her English is very good.She came here one year ago, but she didn't like the weather here.She said she would go to New York to teach Chinese.I know Americans speak English, too.She could find this job, I think.But, I also think that American English is a bit different from Australian English.Anyway, Americans would understand her .And she also speaks good Chinese.Now, in the world, more and more people want to learn more languages.For example, many Americans are learning Chinese.
（一）  语言
  艾丽斯・格林小姐是我们班上的留学生，她来自澳大利亚。她的英语说得很好。一年前她来到这儿，但她不喜欢这儿的天气。她说她要到纽约去教汉语。我知道美国人也是说英语的。我想她在那儿会找到工作的。但是我还是觉得美国英语和澳大利亚的英语是有点儿差别的。不管怎样，美国人是可以听懂她的澳大利亚英语的。而且，她的汉语也说得很好。现在，世界上很多人都想多学一些语种，比如，很多美国人就正在学汉语。
(2)The Importance of English
   There are thousands of languages in the world.Each language seems to be important to the people who speak it as their mother tongue.The importance of a language can be judged by two things.The first is how many people speak that language.The second is how widely the people speaking that language are distributed over the world.No doubt, English is one of the most important languages widely used in the world.People use a language in one of three ways: as a native language, as a second language, or as a foreign language.English is spoken as a native language by almost three hundred million people; as a second language, English is necessary for official business, education, information and other activities in many countries.And it is one of the few "working" languages of the United Nations.It is said that English has become the international language.
（二）英语的重要性
   世界上有成千上万种语言。每一种语言对于把它当成母语来说的人都是重要的。一种语言的重要性可以从两方面来判断。第一，要看说这种语言的人的数量；第二，要看说这种语言的人在世界上是否是广泛分布。毫无疑问，英语是世界上广泛使用的最重要的语言之一。人们一般是通过下面三种方式中的一种来使用语言的：作为母语；作为第二种语言；作为外语。大约有三亿人是将英语作为母语的。而且英语又是很多大国的商业，教育，信息和进行其他活动的必要的第二语言。它还是联合国为数不多的官方工作语言之一。据说，英语已经成为一门国际语言了。

   Yesterday was Sunday.Our classmates called to have a picnic in the park.It was really a good day.The sun was shinning in the sky, the grass was green.The beautiful flowers were greeting us when we saw them.We sat in a circle and brought out what we prepared before.Tom brought beef, Alice brought out her cakes, they were delicious.Bill caught a fish in the lake.We also took a radio and a walkman.We ate, chatted, laughed, played games and listened to music.In the afternoon, an old man came near to us.He seemed to look for something.We said hello to him and asked if he needed our help.He said his wife lost her glasses near the tree.All of us tried to look for and finally found them in the grass.Then, we invited him to sit down with us.He told us many stories that afternoon.We had a very good time!
   一次野餐
   昨天是星期天。我们班同学打电话相约去公园进行野餐。天气非常的好。阳光明媚，绿草茵茵。当我们看见那些美丽的花朵时，它们仿佛也在向我们问好。我们坐成一圈掏出准备好的东西。汤姆带来了牛排，艾丽斯拿出蛋糕，都是非常好吃的。贝尔在湖中钓了一条鱼。我们还带了一台收音机和一个随身听。我们吃着、聊着、笑着，玩游戏，听音乐。在下午的时候，有一个老人朝我们这边走来。他看上去好像在找什么东西，我们向他打招呼，并问他是否需要我们帮忙。他说他妻子的眼镜丢在树旁边了。我们所有的人都找了起来，最后终于在草丛里发现了眼镜。然后我们邀请他和我们坐在一起。那个下午他给我们讲了很多故事。我们玩得非常愉快！
(2)Visiting an Exhibition
   It's Sunday morning.My friend and I are visiting an exhibition.The exhibition hall is magnificent.Many kinds of goods are on display.Thousands of visitors come here every day.A girl is showing some guests around the hall.They are looking at the exhibits and talking about them.In front of the showcases, some young women are chatting about the colors and styles of the dresses.They are especially interested in them.Look, many people are discussing business there.The exhibition is quite successful.
   去展览馆参观
   星期天的早晨。我和我的朋友去展览馆参观。展览馆非常的壮观。陈列了许多物品。每天到这里的参观者数以千计。一个女孩正领着一些客人参观大厅。他们边看展览边谈论着。在陈列柜前，一些年轻妇女聊着服装的颜色和样式。她们对此特别感兴趣。看，那里有许多人在洽谈生意。展览会是非常成功的。
