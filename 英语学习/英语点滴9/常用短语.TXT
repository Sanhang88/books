常用短语

You should give it a try.――你应该试一试

A: I learned to cook western food when I was in the States.
B: It must be interesting.
A: It sure is. You should give it a try. I'll show you.

A：我在美国的时候学会了做西餐。
B：那一定很有趣。
A：当然。你应该试一下。我来教你。

get in somebody's hair――惹某人生气；惹恼某人

Don't talk to her like that, or you'll get in her hair.
不要那样对她说话，你会惹她生气的。
I'm under a lot of pressure.――我的压力很大

A: I'm cracking up. I'm under a lot of pressure at work.
B: Perhaps, you need a vacation.

A：我累极了。我的工作压力很大。
B：也许是，你需要休假。

handle with kid gloves ――小心对待；谨慎

This kind of dispute, we have to handle with kid gloves.
对于这类争端，处理时我们必须谨慎。
We are in the same boat.――我们的处境相同

A: I'm feeling under the weather these days.
B: So am I. I've caught a bad cold.
A: We are in the same boat.

A：这几天我身体不舒服。
B：我也不舒服。我得了重感冒。
A：我们同病相怜。

stand the gaff ――忍受折磨；不怯懦

If you want to succeed in this field, you'll have to stand the gaff.
如果你想在这一行成功，就必须要勇敢的承受磨难。
